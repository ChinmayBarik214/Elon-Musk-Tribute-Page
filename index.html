Elon Musk

the man who aspires to save our planet while getting us a new one to inhabit: genius, visionary; a madcap hybrid of Thomas Edison and Tony Stark

Timeline of Elon Musk's Life so far:

  • 1971 - Born and raised in Pretoria, South Africa.
  • 1983 - Sold the code of a video game he made called Blaster for approximately $500.
  • 1995 - Received a bachelor's degree in economics and physics. Moved to California to attend Stanford University but decided to pursue a business career instead and cofounded Zip2.
  • 1999 - Compaq acquired Zip2 for $307 million. Musk co-founded online bank X.com.
  • 2000 - X.com merged with Confinity to form PayPal.
  • 2001 - Became involved with Mars Society.
  • 2002 - eBay bought PayPal for $1.5 billion, Musk made $165 million. Musk founded SpaceX.
  • 2004 - Musk joined Tesla Motors as chairman and product architect.
  • 2006 - Helped create SolarCity.
  • 2008 - Became CEO of Tesla. SpaceX succeeded in launching the Falcon 1 into orbit. Tesla first built an electric sports car, the Roadster.
  • 2015 - Co-founded OpenAI. SpaceX successfully landed the first stage of a Falcon 9. received an honorary doctorate in engineering and technology.
  • 2016 - Co-founded Neuralink and founded The Boring Company.
  • 2017 - purchased domain X.com from Paypal, explaining it has sentimental value.
  • 2018 - SpaceX launched the Falcon Heavy. The Boring Company sold 2,000 novelty flamethrowers. Musk arranged for his employees to build a small rescue pod to assist the rescue of children stuck in a flooded cavern in Thailand. Musk was elected a fellow of the Royal Society (FRS).
  • 2019 - 60 operational Starlink satellites were launched.
  • 2020 - Tesla became the most valuable carmaker.
  • 2021 - Musk donated $5.7 billion of Tesla's shares to charity. Musk became the first person with a net worth over $300 billion. Musk nominally changed his title to Technoking of Tesla while retaining his position as CEO. Musk was selected as Time's "Person of the Year".

"Person of the Year is a marker of influence, and few individuals have had more influence than Musk on life on Earth, and potentially life off Earth too"

-- Time editor-in-chief Edward Felsenthal
